    • Xpress Electronic Acess & Subscription Services Help

    XEA (Verizon East) and SS (Verizon West) were two different systems enabling wholesale customers
    to provide Verizon service in turn to their customers. This prototype was developed to illustrate how to
    collapse all forms from the two systems into one form for one system. Using JavaScript, I prepopulate
    field data indicating which form was being sent and from which region the request was coming.
